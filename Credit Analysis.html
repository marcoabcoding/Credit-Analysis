Executive Summary

Credit Analysis Overview

The credit application from Alpha College can be approved with conditions, aiming for loan repayment through control of its current debt, thus ensuring the company receives its payments. The decision is based on balancing the growth trajectory the college has shown with its cash generation capacity, indicating possible feasibility in releasing the requested credit. The significant risks associated with its high level of indebtedness, leverage, and history with expenses are factors considered in this analysis.

Strengths

  • Consistent revenue growth: R$ 7.2MM (2022) → R$ 8.5MM (2023) → R$ 9.6MM (2024)
  • Positive cash flow generation: EBITDA R$ 1.1MM
  • Strategic purpose: property acquisition
  • 18-month relationship history with Isaac

Risk Factors

  • High debt level: DSCR 0.49x
  • Supplier protests (R$ 18k)
  • SCR (CNPJ): Existing line of R$ 1MM
  • History of maintainer delays

Questions to Consider

  • What is the student capacity limit at the school? (possible limitation on revenue that has been declining over the years)
  • What is the proportion of students at the beginning and end of the school period? (possible revenue decline if there are many students in the final year of education)
  • What is the value of the rent that will be added to the cash flow? (increase in revenue)
  • Has the school had a history of increasing the average ticket? (possible increase in churn)

Final Decision: Future Conditional Approval

Based on risk and opportunity analysis, we recommend approval of the operation with specific conditions for risk mitigation.

Approval Justification

  • Solid growth trajectory and healthy demand for services
  • Positive cash flow generation (EBITDA R$ 1.1MM)
  • Strategic purpose: property acquisition
  • 18-month relationship history with Isaac

Structural Conditions

  • Real guarantees: fiduciary assignment of the building
  • Financial covenants: minimum DSCR 1.2x
  • Continuous performance monitoring
  • Regularization of pending issues before disbursement
  • Settlement of existing debts: contracted line of R$ 1MM SCR and protests R$ 18k
  • Total debt value not exceeding 35%

Conclusion

Based on detailed analysis, the credit application from Alpha College is recommended for conditional approval with appropriate risk mitigation measures.
